Sushi

Description

This is an easy to follow recipe to make delicious sushi in the comformt of your home. We are going to focus on making a "crunchy roll" type sushi, which cointains the nice crunch of tempura shrimp. This will be complemented with fresh avocado, nori (seaweed), sesame seeds, oyster sauce and more. You can get all of these ingredients at your local supermarket. Lets prepare and get ready to enjoy some delicious sushi!

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice
  • Sushi vinegar
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Tempura shrimp
  • Sesame seeds
  • Nori
  • Eel sauce
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Wash and cook in the ricecooker
  2. Take out tempura shrimp and cook in the oven for 30 min at 400 degrees F
  3. Wash and cut avocado and cucumber
  4. When rice is cooked prepare a large bowl with 3 cups of rice
  5. Add 1/4 cup of sushi vinegar, 3 tablespoons of sesame seeds, and mix wel
  6. Get your nori and add enough rice to thinkly cover the surface
  7. Take the tempura shrimp, avocado, and cucumber to cut it into rectangular pieces and place it in the middle of the the rice in a line.
  8. Roll the nori up so the inside is tightly secured
  9. Use a sharp blade to cut the sushi into rolls
  10. Enjoy your sushi and add as much soy sauce and eel sauce as you like
