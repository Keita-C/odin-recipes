Pizza

Description

Enjoy the taste of Italy. This recipe will cause your tastebuds to be in the heart of Naples. An authentic mozzerella pizza, it will bring the people at your table together. Bon appetit!

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzerella cheese

Steps

  1. Put 2 cups of flour,1/2 cup of water, and a teaspoon of yeast in a large bowl
  2. Mix ingredients well to form the dough
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and place a warm cloth over the bowl
  4. Let the dough rise for 30 min
  5. Use a roller to roll the dough until it forms a circle
  6. Add your desired amount of pizza sauce on the dough as well as the mozzerella
  7. Bake in oven at 500 degrees F for 10 min
  8. Enjoy
