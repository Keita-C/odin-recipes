Curry

Description

Need more flavor in your life? This Indian Curry will spice up your tastebuds. Bland is a thing of the past.

A creamy curry sauce added on top of steamed rice is a meal made to satisfy everyone.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Curry powder
  • Garam masala
  • Garlic powder
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Cumin
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Wash and cook
  2. In a sauce pan cook chicken on low heat
  3. Add curry powder, garam masala, garlic powser, onions, milk, and cumin
  4. Put the lid on and cook for 30 min
  5. Take off lid and put the heat on simmer
  6. Add sauce on rice and enjoy
